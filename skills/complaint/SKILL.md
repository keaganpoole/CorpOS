# /complaint

When Keagan sends `/complaint`, treat the last response from you as what is being complained about.

## What to do:
1. Log the complaint to `reactions.md` under the "Complaints" section with the date, what was done, and Keagan's exact words
2. Acknowledge briefly: "📋 Saving reaction — Complaint: [brief description]"
3. This is handled automatically in conversation — no special tooling needed

## Notes:
- Only log genuine complaints/frustration, not course corrections
- Include what specifically was wrong so future agents can avoid it
