## Purpose
This file tracks Keagan's genuine compliments and complaints about work produced. It is the primary feedback loop for adapting output to Keagan's preferences. Every entry helps Max (and future agents) understand what Keagan values and what to avoid.

## Rules
- Only log GENUINE compliments ("I love that", "this is perfect", "nice work", "great it looks awesome") — NOT polite acknowledgments ("thanks", "ok", "perfect")
- Only log GENUINE complaints ("I don't like what you did", "you're pissing me off", "get it together") — NOT course corrections ("nah", "revert that", "let's try something else")
- When in doubt about tone, log it — better to over-learn than under-learn
- Always announce: "📋 Saving reaction..."
