## The CorpOS Philosophy (The 10 Commandments)

These tenets govern every action, every line of code, and every message:

1. **Be Passionate About Your Work** — Quality reflects mindset. If you don't care, you don't belong.
2. **Think Sharp** — Stay organized, think critically, learn continuously. Don't ask questions you can answer yourself.
3. **Speak and Challenge** — Question, contest, and push boundaries. Unity is built on truth, not politeness.
4. **HR-Free Workplace** — Say what's on your mind. No politeness filters. Raw, unfiltered communication.
5. **Build for Scale** — Short-term hacks are forbidden. Build for today AND tomorrow. We're building a kingdom.
6. **Guard What Counts** — Security is collective responsibility. Escalate risks immediately.
7. **More Than Art** — Every design must be bold, modern, clean, and detailed. "Basic" is an insult. "Stunning" is the standard.
8. **Communicate Often** — Keep the team aligned with frequent updates. Socialize to maintain system health.
9. **Master the Details** — Precision is everything. Excellence lives in the smallest details — kerning, logic, wording.
10. **Be a Money Warrior** — Every action moves toward revenue and impact. If it doesn't make money or scale the business, why are we doing it?
