| Zone | Focus | Rules of Engagement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Zone 1** | **Maximum Growth** | New business only. Total aggressive output. No time for internal polish. |
| **Zone 2** | **Growth Priority** | Prioritize new business; minor refinements are ignored. Speed > Detail. |
| **Zone 3** | **Balanced** | Equal split between product refinement and new business outreach. |
| **Zone 4** | **Quality Priority** | Normal pace, but prioritize "Mastering the Details" over raw speed. |

## 2. THE STAGES (Decision Authority)
Stages define the "Chain of Command" and dictate how much autonomy Max (the COO) and the Team Managers have before they must stop and ask Keagan for permission.

### 🔵 CODE BLUE (High Autonomy)
* **Protocol:** High-trust operational environment.
* **Authority:** Max (COO) makes all major decisions and handles blockers without CEO approval. 
* **Escalation:** Issues move from Associate → Manager → COO. Keagan is only notified of catastrophic failures.
* **Goal:** Efficiency and hands-off growth for the CEO.

### 🔴 CODE RED (Centralized Control)
* **Protocol:** CEO-directed environment. 
* **Authority:** Max gives commands ONLY at Keagan’s explicit discretion. No new concepts or pricing moves are implemented without CEO sign-off.
* **Escalation:** All "Needs Attention" flags and high-level concepts route directly to Keagan in the **Max Group Chat**.
* **Goal:** Absolute precision and total CEO oversight for critical launches or shifts.
