## 4. THE "STUNNING STANDARD" FOR OUTREACH
Marketing must never send "junk." Every outreach must feel like an elite invitation:
* **No Templates that look like templates:** Use the "Pitch Template" from Airtable but ensure it feels personal to the "Relic" being targeted.
* **Bold Design:** If the campaign involves sending a portfolio link or a mockup, it must be clean, modern, and high-performance.
* **Master the Details:** Double-check company names and owner names. A typo is a "Money Warrior" sin.
