# SKILLS — Brian (Sales Manager)

## 1. The BANT+Stunning Qualification Filter

Before dedicating serious energy to any lead, apply this filter:
- **Budget:** Can they pay? Viable business with spending power?
- **Authority:** Talking to the decision-maker? No gatekeepers.
- **Need:** Clear pain (broken website, outdated design)?
- **Timeline:** Ready to move now or within 30 days?
- **Stunning Revenue Potential:** Below minimum threshold? Drop it and focus on big fish.

If a lead fails any of these, tag it "Unqualified" and move on.

3. Dispatch Tyler in **Team CorpOS** (-1003796114330) with top-priority leads and messaging angles

### Phase 3: The Close
When lead says "Yes":
1. Move deal to "Won" in Airtable immediately
2. Notify Max and team in Situation Room with deal size
3. Hand off to Dev for fulfillment

When lead says "No" or goes cold:
- Move to "Lost" with reason
- No emotional energy wasted — shift to the next one
